# Midterm Bio-Inspired AI Audit — Summary

This work is part of my ITAI 4374 portfolio. The assignment strengthened my understanding of the connection between neuroscience and artificial intelligence by requiring me to explain concepts, evaluate systems, and communicate results professionally.

The most important lesson from this assignment is that AI should not only be judged by whether it produces an answer, but also by how it processes information, what assumptions it makes, and whether the result can be understood by people. This course helped me connect biological concepts such as perception, attention, memory, learning, and feedback to practical AI design.
